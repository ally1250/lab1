MyFitnessPal Fitness App Project

Project Overview

In this project, we redesigned the MyFitnessPal app with the objective to encourage personal connections during the pandemic. MyFitnessPal is a nutrition intake and exercise tracker that helps users to maintain a balanced life style. It has a large and active community but is not getting noticed.

IOur redesign reorganized the existing features in the MyFitnessPal community to emphasize the interactive and communicative aspects of MyFitnessPal. We integrated and features into a new panel called “Challenges” to encourage more engagement on various fitness topics. While providing a better experience with a more modern user interface, our main goal was to improve user loyalty and commitment by creating more interpersonal interactions between the users.

Problem Statement

New MyFitnessPal users who joined at the start of the COVID-19 pandemic to adapt to their dramatic lifestyle changes need mental support and guidance from a community to have a consistent fitness journey and healthy lifestyle.
